~Rooted Farmers Market~

We want to build a community who is passionate about creating a sustainable lifestyle. This market was established by and for those who want to teach. learn. grow and become rooted in what our earth has to offer and find ways to give back to our planet.
When: Every 1st & 3rd Friday of the month, 4-8PM
Where: Greenspot Farms 10133 Ward Way Mentone, CA 92359

About

Rooted Farmers Market is co-founded by Alexis Ortiz and Alexandria Contreras. Both of these individuals are life long residents of Redlands, CA and have spent their entire lives dedicating themselves to make their community a better place.
